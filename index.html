Overview

Who are you? What do you specialize in? In today's world, you need your own webpage to establish your brand.

You don't need anything too fancy — just a single page that sums up who you are and links out to all your other sites. Together, we can create a professional site that represents you in less than a week.

Step 1: Initial consultation

Email me to set up a meeting. I promise I don't bite.

Step 2: Pick a template

Go to StyleShout or HTML5 Up and find a single page, resume template that you want. Here are a couple of my favorites:

  • Kards
  • Ceevee
  • Highlights

Step 3: Identify changes

Don't like the color? Some sections don't apply to you? Just let me know.

Step 4: Fill in content

Send me an export of your Linkedin profile. Add additional content and images you want to include on your page.

Step 5: Watch it go up!

I'll package it all together. We can review and make sure we got it all right.
